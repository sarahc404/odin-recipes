Hedgehogs

Hedgehogs are the most delicious snack ever. You should make them immediately.

They're not super healthy, but who cares. Eat and be happy.

Ingredients

  • 397g can condensed milk
  • 250g butter
  • 2 pkts tennis biscuits
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp dessicated coconut
  • 200g chocolate

Steps

  1. Crush biscuits to small pieces.
  2. Mix in cocoa and coconut.
  3. In a separate microwave-proof bowl, melt butter.
  4. Mix condensed milk with melted butter.
  5. Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  6. Place mixture into 2 23cm x 23cm trays and flatten using a spoon.
  7. Place in fridge and allow to set.
  8. Once set, melt chocolate and pour a layer on top of set hedgehog base and place back in fridge to set.
  9. Once set, cut into pieces and place in airtight container.
